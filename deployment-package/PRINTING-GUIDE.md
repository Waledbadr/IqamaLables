## ğŸ“ Label Alignment Tips

### **Test Print First**
1. Load plain paper in your printer
2. Print one page to check alignment
3. Hold printed page over your label sheet
4. Adjust margins if needed

### **Common Adjustments**
- **Labels too high**: Increase top margin
- **Labels too low**: Decrease top margin
- **Labels too far left**: Increase left margin
- **Labels too far right**: Decrease left margin

### **Fine-Tuning**
- Use 0.1mm precision for exact positioning
- Make small adjustments (1-2mm at a time)
- Test print after each adjustment
